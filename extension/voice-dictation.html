HERMES BROWSER EXTENSION

Voice dictation

Chromium can block microphone capture inside extension side panels. This visible extension tab records from the same Hermes extension origin when Hermes STT is available, or uses Browser speech fallback when the connected runtime does not expose audio transcription.

No audio is saved by this page. In Hermes STT mode, audio is sent once to your configured Hermes transcription endpoint when you stop recording. In Browser speech fallback mode, only the transcript is sent back to the side panel.

Click Start dictation to grant microphone access and record.
